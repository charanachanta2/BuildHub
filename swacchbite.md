# 🚀 SwacchBite Frontend Website

## 🎯 Objective
Build a clean and modern **frontend landing page** for a startup called **SwacchBite**.

SwacchBite focuses on improving **street food hygiene and safety** through certification and digital audits.

---

## 🧠 Project Description

Create a visually appealing homepage using the following content:

---

### 🏷 Brand Name
**SwacchBite**

---

- Title: *Certified Street Food, Trusted Taste.*
- Subtitle: *Modernizing street food safety through rigorous audits and digital certification.*
- CTA Button: *Order Now!*

---

#### 📖 About Section
Explain:
- What SwacchBite does
- Why food safety is important
- How it helps vendors and customers

---

#### 🛠 Services Section
Include:
- Vendor certification
- Hygiene audits
- Digital tracking system
