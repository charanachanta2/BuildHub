Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters to BuildHub

At BuildHub, we're committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, full name, and profile data
  • Project Data: Code submissions, project descriptions, and learning progress
  • Activity Data: Achievements, badges earned, and activity history
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser information, and usage patterns

2. Authentication & Security

We use secure third-party authentication providers (Google, GitHub) to verify your identity. Your passwords are encrypted using industry-standard practices, and we implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Operate your account and provide platform access
  • Display your personalized dashboard and progress
  • Award badges and track achievements
  • Send important notifications and updates
  • Improve our platform features and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your project work and profile may be visible to other users on the platform depending on your privacy settings.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyze platform usage. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Your continued use of BuildHub constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@buildhub.io

BuildHub is committed to your privacy and data protection.
